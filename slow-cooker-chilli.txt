Ingredients
-----------
128g uncooked quinoa
1 medium red or yellow onion, peeled and diced
4 cloves of garlic, minced
2 400g tins dark red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 400g tin aldi taco beans
2 tubes tomato puree
2 400g tins diced tomatoes
500ml vegetable stock
330ml can beer
1 tsp ntsama chilli paste (for mild)
1 Tablespoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper


Method
------
- Put all ingredients in slow cooker, cook for 5 hours on high


Serving suggestions
-------------------
Tortilla chips
