Ingredients
-----------
800g root vegetables
2 medium onions, thinly sliced
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2 tbsp ginger, finely chopped
25g bunch of coriander
2 green chillies thinly sliced
1 tbsp tomato puree
2 tsp methi blend
1 tsp haldi blend
1 tsp mirchi blend

Method
------
- Fry onion in 3 tbsp oil for 10 minutes or until soft
- Stir in vegetables, garlic, 1 tsp methi blend, the haldi and mirchi blend, 1 tsp salt then cook for 10 minutes.
- Stir in 200ml water and tomato puree, cover and cook for 40 minutes (add a splash of water if pan dires out too much).
- Sprinkle with 1 tsp methi blend, ginger, coriander and green chillies
